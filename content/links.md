## Notable Projects and Blogs {#notable-projects-and-blogs}


### [Reverse Engineering for Beginners](https://beginners.re/) {#reverse-engineering-for-beginners}

Not just for beginners, also good when you want to learn something new. Dennis
is one of the Best guys out there. Very approachable when you have reversing
questions.


### [Gwern Branwen's blog](https://gwern.net/) {#gwern-branwen-s-blog}

Pseudonymous polymath's blog exploring AI, security, darknet markets, and
computational theory with obsessive rigor and extensive research. Essential
for understanding the intersection of exploit development, machine learning,
and weird machines.


### [OpenBSD](http://openbsd.org) {#openbsd}

Security-focused operating system prioritizing correctness and proactive
defense over performance. Known for hardened kernel, secure defaults,
extensive code audits, and mitigations like ASLR, DEP, and stack canaries that
became industry standard. Essential for understanding modern OS-level exploit
defenses.
